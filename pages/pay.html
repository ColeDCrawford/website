Pay MITOC

This page can be used to pay money to MITOC using PayPal (preferred) or a credit/debit card. Payments can also be made by check at the MITOC office during office hours. All payments are final and non-refundable.

If you are having trouble with the payment system, contact mitoc-bursar@mit.edu. The most common issue is that the billing address must exactly match what your bank has on file, so if your card is declined, double-check the address you entered.

If you are an MIT student and this cost is prohibitive to your membership/participation, please email mitoc-owner@mit.edu to ask about this fee being waived. If you are a non-MIT student please feel free to email to ask about financial assistance options.
