MITOC strives to provide at-cost gear rentals for members. Simply drop by when the office (W20-461) is open. You must leave a deposit check of equivalent value as the gear you are renting with us.

You are charged by the number of days you use gear. There is a minimum of 1 rental day for each weekend you have the gear. You may rent gear for up to 10 weeks.

MITOC reserves the right to cash your deposit check if the gear is not returned after 10 weeks and 3 email attempts to contact you.

Restricted Gear

Due to the nature of climbing/mountaineering safety and gear maintenance, some gear is restricted. Some gear is restricted to club trips (not listed), others must get gear manager/climbing chair approval. To inquire about renting restricted gear, contact the approval contact below.
