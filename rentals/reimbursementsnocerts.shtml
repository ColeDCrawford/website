REIMBURSEMENT (no MIT certificates)

Does MITOC owe you money? Follow these directions to get reimbursed. You must submit the physical copy of your receipt to the MITOC office.

SAO requires a physical and electronic copy of your receipt and we need a quick description for our records. Do all 3 components below.

1. Electronic receipt (PDF):

  1. Hard copy receipts - scan them! There is a scanner in the office. Online order receipt - save pdf copy.
  2. Attach receipts to email:
    To: mitoc-trez@mit.edu, yourself@wahoo.com
    Subject: Reimbursement: [what money was for (ex. Camelot keyholder trip, circus gas, WFA)]
    Body: Your name and mailing address

2. Physical receipt:

  1. Grab Coversheet for all hardcopies of receipts. Fill out 2 lines (circled):
    • Payee's name: (your name)
    • Contact Information: (your email)
  2. Staple your receipts to the Coversheet, put in colorful "Reimbursement" envelope in upper safe

3. MITOC Records

Please fill out 1 form per receipt. If you have a large receipt (over $100) with multiple types of purchases (say a BD order with both skis and climbing gear) it would be much better if you broke those into 2 forms rather than lumping the whole purchase into 1 category (climbing or skiing).

If you're are applying for leader subsidy, lump all receipts together and just use 1 form.
