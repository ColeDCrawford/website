CABINS

MITOC has two cabins in New Hampshire, Camelot and Intervale.

IMPORTANT UPDATES ON INTERVALE CABIN - TO USE INTERVALE, YOU MUST READ THE ENTIRE INTERVALE PAGE HERE AND ABIDE BY THE POLICIES SET FORTH BY THE CABIN MANAGERS OR RISK LOSING YOUR INTERVALE USAGE PRIVILEDGES!

Who can use the cabin?

  • MITOC members with keyholder training can rent cabin keys from our office.
  • All MITOC members can stay at the yurt (Intervale) or lean-to (Camelot), or camp on the property.
You must always contact the cabin manager before staying at the cabin, or camping on MITOC property.

Who is a keyholder and how do I become one?

Keyholders are MITOC members who have attended a training session and work day at the cabin. Keyholder trainings are held a few times throughout the year and are announced on the MITOC list. You can also become a keyholder by helping a manager around the cabin. Managers will email cabin-helpers mailing list whenever they need an extra hand. Be sure to add yourself on that mailing list if you're interested.

I'm not a keyholder, can I still stay at the cabin?

  • Check the calendar to the left and see if there will be a keyholder staying at the cabin.
  • Email the keyholder requesting if you can stay with them. Cc the cabin manager.

Can I just camp or stay at the yurt/lean-to?

Yes, if you are a MITOC member. Email the cabin manager asking if you can camp on the property or stay at the yurt/lean-to.
