Sleeping gear in the loft

Note: The sleeping bags in the loft are old, old MITOC bags. Some are fit only for padding or as a summer-only bag, others are in decent shape, but have lost 10 or more degrees from their original rating. They don't receive much maintanence (ie, washing). Use at your own discretion. It's recommended you bring your own bag or rent a new bag from MITOC.
