Directions to the MIT Outing Club's Camelot Cabin.

Camelot is located in 1238 North Groton Rd,
Rumney NH 03266

From Boston: 125 miles, 2.25 - 3 hrs driving

  • I-93 North, or Rt 3 to I-93 North, or Rt 1 to I-93 North via Tobin Bridge.
  • Exit 26, just past Plymouth, NH, to Rt 25 West.
  • Follow Rt 25 west for 8.5 miles; past Hatch Plaza Stores (where there is a large supermarket with good prices and a decent bakery) (turn at next intersection for nearest hospital); past traffic circle at Rt 3A; past two pairs of yellow blinking lights by Rumney.
  • Look for yellow warning sign for intersection of Hall's Brook Road. Slow down and get ready to turn left immediately (there will be a + four way intersection, then the next sign with be a -| three way intersection sign, which is what you want). Turn left onto Hall's Brook Road.
  • Proceed for approx 3 mi uphill to town of North Groton. Hall's Brook Rd is very windy and bumpy, at times there is a fairly deep stream bed on the left.
  • Turn right at North Groton towards Cheever (Hall's Brook Rd ends at a "T" in North Groton).
  • Go 1.1 miles from intersection at North Groton, past the cemetary on the left, up the hill past several houses.
  • Parking area is on the right, halfway up the hill (the road is curving slightly to the right). The trail leaves from the downhill corner of the parking lot and is about 100m to the cabin. The trail goes over the bridge crossing the stream.
  • If there is cut wood left near the parking lot, please help carry some back to the cabin's woodshed every time you come from your car.

Slippery roads?? Miss the turn at Hall's Brook Road??

  • Continue west on Rt 25 for an additional 2.9 miles (11.3 miles from I-93), past rest area.
  • Junction 118 (to I-89): turn left at small store, go south on 118 for 2.0 miles
  • Cheever: the white Cheever Union Chapel will appear suddenly on your right at the top of a hill after some trees.
  • Turn left after the last building onto North Groton Rd. (after you see this sign), and then stay on your right immediately after then turn; avoid taking the smaller left fork (it goes up to Bailey Hill).
  • Follow the road for 2.6 miles, steadily uphill.
  • Halfway down the first downhill slope, look for the parking area on the left as described above.

From the west:

  • I-91 North to White River Jct VT/Lebanon NH
  • US-4 East to Canaan (near Cardigan, a nice Sunday afternoon hike)
  • Rt 118 North to Cheever
  • See above directions from Cheever.
