EXCLUSIVE RENTAL

We offer the MITOC cabins for rent by groups for retreats/events. Reserving an exclusive rental gives your group exclusive access to the cabin for the days you rent. This means no other parties may stay at the cabin or property during your stay.

The exclusive rental rate for Camelot is $300, and Intervale $250 per night. You must bring your exclusive reservation fee check (and cabin security deposit of $1000) to office hours in order to reserve the cabin. First come first serve. We cannot guarantee exclusive rental until we have your checks (not the case for non exclusive rentals). The keyholder may cancel the reservation no later than 7 days before the reservation date without penalty. Less than 7 days and we will cash the exclusive reservation fee check. No exceptions!
