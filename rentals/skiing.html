MITOC strives to provide at-cost gear rentals for members. Simply drop by when the office is open. You must leave a deposit check of equivalent value as the gear you are renting with us. You will pay for your rental with a check when you return the item.

You are charged by the number of days you use gear. There is a minimum of 1 rental day for each weekend you have the gear. You may rent gear for up to 10 weeks after which you will be notificed by MITOC via email 3 times about the late rental.

MITOC reserves the right to cash your deposit check if the gear is not returned after 10 weeks and 3 email attempts to contact you.

Deposit amounts vary by item. Average deposit is listed below.
