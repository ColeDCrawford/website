Spring Circus 1997
Back to History Page

Each year the Outing Club organizes a Spring Circus on a long weekend in the Spring. The group generally travels out to the MIT cabin at Camelot, where people participate in a variety of outdoors activities, including canoeing, hiking, and rock climbing. There always hikes and climbs for outdoorsmen/women of all skill levels. Beginners are especially encouraged to use Spring Circus as an introduction to the Outing Club and the activities that it offers.
