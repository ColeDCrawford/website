Current Trip Schedule

Each year the Outing Club organizes a Fall Circus on anywhere from Late September to October. The group generally travels out to the MIT cabin at Camelot, where people participate in a variety of outdoors activities, including canoeing, hiking, and rock climbing. There always hikes and climbs for outdoorsmen/women of all skill levels. Beginners are especially encouraged to use Fall Circus as an introduction to the Outing Club and the activities that it offers.
