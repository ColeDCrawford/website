What are Office Hours?

Office hours are a time you can:
  • Ask questions about MITOC
  • Become a member
  • Rent gear, cabin keys, or boathouse keys
  • Submit payment for a MITOC trip or course
  • Submit reimbursments
  • Buy MITOC gear

If you have rental related questions, check Rental Policies.

Remember : Don't forget your checkbook!

When?

Regular office hours are at 7pm-8pm on Monday and Thursday. See the calendar for holiday hours and additional hours.
