Donations

Donations are much appreciated. Donations may be made directly to MITOC or through MIT Student Activites.

Donating Directly to MITOC

Please drop by our office hours or mail a check made out to MITOC to:

MIT Outing Club
84 Massachusetts Ave
PO Box 397119
Cambridge, MA 02139-4300

Donations directly to MITOC may specifically go to cabins, specific gear, winter school, climbing, bouldering wall, or other, please email mitoc-trez@mit.edu.

Donating Through MIT

Please mail a check made out to MITOC to:

MIT Recording Secretary
MIT Room W98-308
600 Memorial Drive
Cambridge, MA 02139

Donations may be considered tax-deductible only when routed through the MIT Treasurer's Office. Please refer to the Student Activties (SAO) Website for more details.

Donating Gear

MITOC also accepts gear donations. Please drop by our office hours to donate gear. Please email mitoc-owner@mit.edu for any questions.
