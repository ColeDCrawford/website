Cape in a Day Plus One
Sat-Sun, June 19-20
Shari (617) 258-1814
Bicycle 100 miles from Boston to N. Truro or Eastham on Saturday, tour Provincetown or relax on nearby beaches Sunday, and take the ferry back to Boston late Sunday afternoon. The cost of $75 includes four meals, lots of snacks, hostel lodging, sag wagon support, the ferry and a T-shirt. Send full payment (refundable until June 1st) along with address, phone # and hostel choice (if any) to trip leader Seth Davis, 186 Palmer St., Arlington, MA 02474. For more information, call Shari at (617) 258-1814 or send SASE to the trip leader. HI-AYH trip.

Intro Circus '99
Fri-Sun, September 17-19
mfreed@mit.edu or rzeitham@mit.edu.
Join MITOC for a weekend of outdoor activities after the first full week of classes! This is a great opportunity to meet other members, find out what they do, and spend two days at a cabin in the White Mountains, NH. Here are more details:

WHAT: Hiking, kayaking, canoeing, rock-climbing, biking.
WHERE: Camelot cabin, NH. The cabin acts as a basecamp for everyone while MITOC leaders run day-trips in a multitude of activities.
HOW MUCH: $35/person, plus MITOC membership. Cost covers Transportation (drivers reimbursed for gas), Breakfasts, Dinner, Equipment, and Lodging at Camelot.
