LEAD TRS

Who leads TRS?

Technical Review Sessions (TRS) are review sessions not a lecture. Generally session leaders have taken the course related to the topic reviewed or are presenting miscellaneous topics of interest. As a leader, you won't be expected to know everything on the topic but merely to guide a review session or discussion. If you're feeling a bit fuzzy on a topic because you took the course a while ago, TRS is a great opportunity to review the material in preparation for a running a session.

How to lead TRS?

Simply contact the TRS chair to schedule a session or add yourself to the leaders mailing list.

We encourage leaders to make the session as interactive as possible such as practicing medical scenarios for first aid or practicing rope work for self resuce/mountaineering/climbing.

This is a great way to practice skills that would otherwise be hard to retain after just a 1 weekend course. It's also a great way to meet fellow MITOCers outside the mailing list and large trips.
