BECOME A DESK WORKER

MITOC is made possible by office hours and office hours are made possible by wonderful volunteer desk workers. Give back to the community and become a desk worker!

Who can be a desk worker?

Anyone who...
  • Is generally available Wednesday and Thursday around 6-8pm
  • Is excited about gear, the outdoors, and MITOC!

How and when can I be trained to work desk?

Simply contact the desk captain to arrange to come by when they're working desk. Training doesn't take long and you learn most of it while working.

We have mass training sessions when there's a high demand for desk workers (winter school and summer). Add yourself to any of our mailing lists to stay informed.

What are the benefits of working desk?

  • Be the face of MITOC and help out members excited about going outdoors
  • Giving back to the MITOC community which wouldn't be possible without office hours
  • Free/discounted gear rentals
