Summer Gear List

Not to confuse the issue, but here is a less intimidating gearlist appropriate only for summer backpacking in more-or-less non-bear country, rather than the more general list available in this directory.

Clothing

  • hat -- soft, flexible, broad brim
  • shirt - polypropylene
  • t-shirt - cotton
  • jacket - windbreaker
  • jacket / vest -- warm
  • sweater -- wool or fleece
  • trousers - long; no cuffs
    • - comfortably loose, big pockets
  • shorts (optional)
  • socks - 4 pairs: two inner, two outer
    • - inner socks polypro or thin wool - outer sock thick wool
  • bandana (sunburn protection)
  • swimsuit
  • underclothing
  • rain gear - poncho
  • boots - where them for 6 weeks before trip
  • gaitors (optional)
  • clothing for the ride home (optional)
  • light tent OR ( mosquito net and lean-to PU plastic )
  • ground sheet
  • foam or air mattress
  • warm sleeping bag (expect -5 C)

    • Cooking

  • aluminum pot (or light steel)
  • skillet 10" teflon OR another pot
  • one water pail (gallon icecream bucket)
  • spatula
  • scrubby cloth
  • single burner stove
  • fuel
  • cutlery
    • - cup (plastic) - spoon (metal) - pocket or side knife - bowl (plastic)

    Equipment

  • backpack
  • water bottles - 2 L volume total
  • knife
  • flashlight (powerful enough to navigate in spruce forest at night)
  • spare bulb and batteries for flashlight
  • map and compass
  • candle
  • waterproof lighter and matches
  • 50 ft of rope / cord (1/8 inch)
  • litter bag
  • washcloth; soap (pure glycerine is best; avoid medicated / fragrant / coloured soap); comb
  • toothbrush and paste / powder
  • 1/2 roll toilet paper
  • medication; toiletries
  • insect repellant (compact liquid / roll-on)
  • sunscreen; vaseline
  • tough garbage bag (to pull over pack at night)
  • whistle, camera (extra film), fishing equipment
  • watch, notebook and pencil, playing cards
  • wallet, book,

    • Group Gear

  • repair kit - sewing kit, plastic tape, safety pins, snare wire
  • first aid kit
