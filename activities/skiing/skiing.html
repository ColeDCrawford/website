SKIING

MITOC started as a bunch of skiers, and sking is still an important winter activity in the club. MITOC runs cross country and backcountry ski trips during Winter School and more trips throughout winter. Join the skiing mailing list to stay in informed of upcomming events.

The club is most active in cross country skiing and backcountry skiing, and we rent skis for both. MITOC doesn't do downhill alpine skiing or rent alpine skis, but Snowriders serve the MIT community in this area.
