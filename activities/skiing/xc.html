CROSS COUNTRY SKIING

If you enjoy cross-country skiing, check out: www.xcskiworld.com which tries to cover everything related to cross-country skiing. Of particular interest may be their information on waxing your skis.

If you're the more competitive sort, check out the New England Nordic Ski Association's site. They are a comprehensive competition organizer for New England and link to local clubs.
