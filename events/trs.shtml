TECHNICAL REVIEW SESSIONS

These are weekly meetings on Tuesday at 7pm in the MITOC office. We recommend joining the mitoc mailing list or adding the google calendar to stay informed. We meet weekly to biweekly to review and practice wilderness first aid, self rescue, mountaineering, and climbing skills. These sessions are a great way to refresh and practice skills which are easily forgotten after taking the respective courses. We aim to cover a scenario/skill at each session such as rescuing a follower with a ledge or without a ledge or for first aid run a few scenarios plus discussion. Sessions are run by 2 people who have taken a course or would like to share their experiences. Please fill out the form below if you're interested in running a session!

Example Topics

Here is a noncomprehensive list example of topics that may be covered.

First aid

  • Splinting
  • Cold weather emergencies

Self Rescue/Climbing

  • Rescuing a follower
  • Rescuing a leader
  • Escaping a belay

Mountaineering

  • Glacier travel
  • Avy practice
