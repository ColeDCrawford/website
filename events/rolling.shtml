KAYAK ROLLING

This is a introduction to whitewater kayaking. These indoor pool sessions will prepare people with minimal kayaking experience to take on class II and III rivers in the spring. There will be: MITOC paddling section organizes kayak whitewater and rolling basics for the MIT community. Sessions are designed to prepare you to take up on class II-III rivers in spring and consist of paddling basics and rolling drills spread through six pool classes through February and March 2011 and a kayak theory class.
